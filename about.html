About Us

Hello, we are the All About Animals team!

We are a three man group from Boston University learning how to create websites. The team includes Mathew Folan, Liam Murray, and Miguel Franco. Although we've only just met, we all have similar backgrounds. We grew up outside, in the forests of the world, making friends with all types of animals. Talking with these animals, we have gotten to know them very well and they even told us some fun facts about themselves! So, we wanted to let the whole world about these animal fun facts and decided that a website is the best way to achieve this.
